A Sad Cartoon - Loathe

Loathe is a metal band from Liverpool that create dark and melodic songs. This album in specific, "I Let It in and It Took Everything," was one where they were experimenting more with their sound, as when they first started, they were more known to make ambient music. "A Sad Cartoon" is one of the songs that they claimed help them gain confidence to experiment with new sound.

Milk - Sweet Trip

Released in 2009, the album "You Will Never Know Why," is marked as Sweet Trip's third album released. Though it falls more in the shoegaze category, it is a noteworthy record to mention. "Milk" is about a dispute between a couple, one that has insomnia and one without it. The insomniac partner makes sarcastic remarks of how they're unbothered with their partners ease to drift to sleep. I interpret it as someone that can't get over a lost partner. The constant image of their face appearing in their dream, while they have drifted away. Though they keep telling themselves that they "don't mind," they wish their lost partner hasn't left.
