L$D - A$AP Rocky

A euphoric melody with artist A$AP Rocky discussing his love for indulging in drugs, such as LSD. Despite his mentor A$AP Yams unfortunately dying to a drug overdose, Rocky remains active in drug-participating, catching the out-of-body experience.

After Hours - The Weeknd

A song meant as an apology to a past partner for his mistakes and cause of anguish. The Weeknd poured his soul in the album "After Hours." After years of production and songwriting. The Weeknd was able to break out of homelessness and poverty and shine as one of the most listened to artist in the world. This song serves as the title track to the album, where The Weeknd discusses his toxic habits that caused the end of his relationship. Now he confesses his sins and asks for forgiveness on a very dark and moody instrumental, similar to that of his old work.
