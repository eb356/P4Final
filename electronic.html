you once said my name for the first time - 2hollis

2hollis has only recently been getting attention in the music industry, however he has been in the industry for a while. With his mother being a manager for popular dubstep artist Skrillex, 2hollis love to music would come as no surprise, especially in the electronic genre. His album, "boy," starts with the opening track, "you once said my name for the first time," a track that discusses the pain and agony that comes with letting go of a person. The moments they share right from the beginning are beautiful, but eventually, they will same each others names for the last time.

Suffocation - Crystal Castles

Crystal Castles is an electronic duo that formed in 2004 by Ethan Kaith and Alice Glass. Unfortunately, Alice Glass left the duo as she was being abused by Ethan Kaith. Even throughout the abuse, Alice managed to create several albums, each of them sharing noisy and electronic-clashing instrumentals. "Suffocation," comes from their album "(II)." It features themes of depression and suicide over an etheral instrumental. Some say this song, along with others, may have been Alice Glass' attempt at a cry for help. Fortunately, she's now solo and creates her own songs.
